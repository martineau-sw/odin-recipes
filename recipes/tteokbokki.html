Tteokbokki

Description

Korean dish made with rice cakes, fish cakes, and egg, seasoned with chili paste makes for a tender treat that is very spicy!

Ingredients

  • Water, 3 cups
  • 2 Dried Anchovies
  • Chili Paste, 3 tablespoons
  • White Sugar, 2 tablespoons
  • Soy sauce, 1 tablespoon
  • Corn syrup, 1 tablespoon
  • 2 Korean fish cakes, sliced
  • 1/2 Onion, thickly sliced
  • 1 Spring Onion, thickly sliced

Directions

  1. Combine water and anchovies in a saucepan and bring to a boil. Cook for 10 minutes. Remove anchovies.
  2. Combine chili paste, sugar, soy sauce, and corn syrup into a bowl for the sauce.
  3. Add rice cakes and onion to the saucepan. Add sauce. Bring to a boil and cook for 5 minutes, stirring occasionally. Add spring onion and boil for 3 minutes.
