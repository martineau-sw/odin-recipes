Steps

  • Air Fryer

    1. Set air fryer to 360°F.
    2. Remove sandwich from box, over-wrap and cardboard.
    3. Cook for 16 minutes for one sandwich and 18 minutes for two sandwiches.
    4. Let cool. Enjoy!

  • Microwave

    1. PREP INSTRUCTIONS

      • SLEEVELESS
      • Unwrap sandwich and place on a microwave safe plate.
      • WITH SLEEVE
      • Unwrap sandwich, insert into crisping sleeve, and place on microwave safe plate.

    2. COOKING TIMES on high

      • 1100 Watt Oven
      • 1 Sandwich: 1 minute 50 seconds
      • 2 Sandwiches: 3 minutes 30 seconds
      • Lower Wattage/Compact Microwave
      • 1 Sandwich: 2 minutes, 50 seconds
      • 2 Sandwiches: For best results, cook one sandwich at a time
